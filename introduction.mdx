## Who Atlas is for

Atlas is built for developers and product teams at banks, NBFCs, lending fintechs, and insurance companies processing documents and financial data at scale — and need structured, reliable, explainable outputs without building the intelligence layer themselves.

If you're building a loan origination flow, an onboarding journey, a credit underwriting pipeline, or a KYC/KYB process — Atlas gives you the document and financial intelligence layer as APIs.

## **What you can do with Atlas**

- Verify and extract data from 54 document types — identity, business, financial, and regional documents including handwritten and low-quality scans
- Detect document fraud — tamper detection, copy-paste flags, digital overlay detection, face-to-ID match, signature consistency
- Analyse bank statements, GST filings, and financial statements for income consistency, cash flow patterns, and fraud signals
- Match addresses and validate business presence across documents
- Extract authorisation matrices from board resolutions
- Get confidence scores and explainable outputs on every result — not black box decisions
